Faith Elizabeth Hale

is a 22 year old photographer out of Northern Colorado. She got her start in 2019 working as the Head photographer of the local student paper. Faith then became the official photographer of the Indian Peaks Girls Softball Association. While workimg with the IPGSA, faith was a finalist in the Run for Art competition sponsored by the Fondazione Guilio Onesti Academia Olympica Nationale Italiana Non-Profit. Faith worked with Yellowscene magazine to capture vignettes of local culture and foods in the Denver Boulder area.
